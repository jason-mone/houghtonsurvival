About

Are you ready to save your loved ones? This guide is how to make the ultimate survival food. The food is called pemmican. It's food from the 18th and 19th centuries originally made by indigenous peoples of North America and then used by voyagers, frontiersmen and explorers alike. It is easy to learn and easy to prepare. Let’s get started.
